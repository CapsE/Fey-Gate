School: Arcane / Elemental
Type: Cone
Range: 10ft.
Casting Time: 2
Concentration: yes

Dozens of small lightning strikes jump from your fingers to the creatures and ground in front of you.

Every creature in the target area takes 1d6 lightning damage. While concentrating on this spell you may spend an action to repeat this effect if you succeed your spell casting check. On a failed check the spell ends.
