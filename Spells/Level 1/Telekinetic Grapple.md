School: Arcane
Type: Debuff
Range: 60ft.
Casting Time: 1
Duration: 1 Minute
Concentration: yes

If your spellcasting check beat 10 + the targets Strength or Dexterity modifier (pick the higher one) the target can't move until it breaks free (trying to beat your arcana) or the spell ends. It also looses 1d4 resolve. While concentrating on this spell you may spend an action to move the target 10ft. in any direction but up.
