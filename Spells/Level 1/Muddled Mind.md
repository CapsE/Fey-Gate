School: Arcane
Type: Debuff
Range: 60ft.
Casting Time: 3
Duration: 1 Minute
Concentration: yes

You whisper soft words into a slight breeze causing a creature to loose focus and let it's mind wander.

The target looses 2d6 resolve. If it can't loose enough resolve it's stunned until it gets attacked, takes damage, it's shaken awake by it's allies, or the spell ends.
