You are capabable of casting magical spells either manipulating your enemies or the battleground to your advantage.

### Scaling
* Health increases a tiny bit
* Resolve increases quite a bit
* Gets awesome spells

##### Spell Casting
You may cast spells and cantrips with the Arcane or Elemental type. Whenever you spend an action casting a none cantrip spell make an Arcana-Skill check if you don't beat a 10 + the spells level you loose 1d4 resolve. If you can't loose resolve the spell fails.
