## Morning Rituals
__Tend to Equipment:__ You gain +1 on attack rolls until the beginning of your next rest.

### Scaling
* Hit-Chance increases to up to 50%
* Damage starts high but doesn't grow much
* Health increases more than other classes
* Armor-Ranking increases
* Resolve increases a bit
* Restore starts strong and increases a bit

##### Endurance
You may add your Athletics Skill modifier to your Restore-checks.

#### Skilled Fighter
You gain +1 to any weapon skill at first level and every time you level up
