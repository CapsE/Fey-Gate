You are trained with weapons and armour and used to keeping a clear head in the midst of any fight. You also motivate and inspire your allies.

### Scaling
* Crit-Chance increases to up to 50%
* Damage starts high but doesn't grow much
* Health increases more than other classes
* Armor-Ranking increases
* Resolve increases a bit
* Restore starts strong and increases a bit

##### Endurance
You may add your Athletics Skill modifier to your Restore-checks.
