Not only are captains strong fighters wielding heavy weapons and armor but their great leaders too. Some might turn to intimidation, running a hard regiment to get the best out of their allies while others inspire their followers to heroic deeds.
Captains usually don't use magic but their superior battle tactics can shape the outcome of a fight just as much.

## Morning Rituals
1. __Plan for the Day:__ You lay out the plan for today for everone and remind them of the important tasks ahead. You and each of your allies resting with you may reroll a d20 check once until the beginning of your next rest.
2. __Motivating Speech:__ Your allies gain +1d6 maximum resolve until the beginning of your next rest.
3. __Tend to Equipment:__ You gain +1 on attack rolls until the beginning of your next rest.

## Level 1
__Battle Cry (Once/Fight):__ You learn one of the following battle cries. You learn an additional battle cry each time you level up. You may only use one of your known battle cries per fight. All battle cries affect allies within 30ft. of you.

#### Battle Cries
1. __Charge:__ Allies may move once at the start of their turn without spending an action.
2. __Stand your ground:__ Allies regain 2d4 resolve.
3. __Hold the Line:__ You order your allies to hold their position. Allies within 30ft. of you that didn't move on their turn regain 4d4 resolve.
4. __Give them Hell:__ Allies may attack one more time until the end of their turn.
5. __Keep them Busy:__ Allies may attack enemies that leave their meele combat range using their reaction
6. __Regroup:__ Allies that use their move action and end their move next to an ally regain 4d4 resolve.
7. __Dispurse:__ Allies that use their move action and do not end their move next to an ally regain 3d4 resolve.
8. __Incomming:__ When allies would loose resolve through ranged attacks they loose only half as much.
## Level 2
__Shouts:__ You have enough overview of the battle field to help out specific allies. You may use your reaction to shout a helpful tip or warning to an ally within 30ft. of you using one of the following shouts.
1. __Watch out:__ The ally gains +5 to their next saving throw
2. __Hurry:__ The ally gains 10ft. of movement speed until the end of their turn
3. __Go for the Knees:__ If the ally hits with their next attack it deals double damage to resolve
4. __Go for the Head:__ If the ally hits with their next attack it deals double damage to HP
5. __Get over here:__ If the ally ends their move next to you, you and the ally regain 1d4 resolve

## Level 3
__Charismatic Leader:__ You may add your charisma to all dice rolls made for Battle Cries and Shouts
