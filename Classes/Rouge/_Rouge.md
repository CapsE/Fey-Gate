You fight dirty and sneaky, avoiding your enemies defences while staying out of sight.

## Subclasses
* [[Assasin]]
* [[Silvertounge]]
* [[Thief]]

### Scaling
* Crit-Chance increases slightly
* Amount of ignored resolve increases slightly
* Damage starts low and doesn't grow much
* Crit-Damage increases massively
* Health increases slightly
* Resolve increases a quite a bit
* Restore increases a bit

##### Precise Strikes
You may ignore one resolve when attacking.

##### Dodge
When you loose a resolve by avoiding damage you can spend your reaction to instantly move 5ft.

##### Trip
When an enemy moves from a position 5ft. from you to another position 5ft. from you, even in passing you can spend your reaction to trip them, loosing two resolve or falling prone.

##### Sneak Attack
When you attack an enemy that hasn't noticed you, you ignore all of it's resolve.
