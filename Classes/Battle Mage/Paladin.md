Paladins are holy warriors that use heavy armor and divine magic to smite their enemies. They are incredibly powerful and capable of taking on even the toughest of foes. Paladins are often seen as the guardians and protectors of the weak and innocent. They are also strong healers, capable of restoring the health of their allies in the heat of battle. Paladins have a strong belief in justice and truth and will always fight for these virtues. Their faith gives them access to powerful divine spells, making them a formidable force in any encounter.
