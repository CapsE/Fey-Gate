Battle Mages are powerful warriors who combine the use of weapons and armor with the power of magic. They are agile, strong, and skilled in both martial and magical combat. They specialize in a wide range of magical abilities, ranging from powerful offensive spells to beneficial defensive spells and even summoning allies to aid them in battle. Their wide array of weapons and armor further increase their versatility and effectiveness in combat. Battle Mages are a deadly force to be reckoned with and their presence on the battlefield is always a formidable one.
