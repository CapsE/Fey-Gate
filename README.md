# Fey-Gate TTRPG

Fey-Gate is a tabletop role-playing game (TTRPG) that is designed as an open source alternative to Dungeons and Dragons. It is still in development, and we need the help of the community to make it the best game it can be! Once Fey-Gate is complete, it will offer an immersive gaming experience with a high fantasy setting, offering players a plethora of exciting locations from bustling cities to long lost ruins in remote deserts. 
Players will be able to customize their characters by choosing from a variety of character classes and builds to fit their playstyle. Fey-Gate will also be a story-driven experience, with improvisation strongly encouraged, letting players craft their own unique adventures. 

Join us in making Fey-Gate the game we know it can be!
