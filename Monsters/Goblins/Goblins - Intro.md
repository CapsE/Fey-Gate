Goblins are small, green-skinned humanoids known for their cunning and vicious nature. They typically live in underground caves, mines and tunnels, and are skilled in the art of trap making. They are also known for their proficiency in crafting crude weapons and armor. Goblins are typically organized in tribes led by a strong chief and often worship dark powers. They are opportunistic raiders, attacking small villages and isolated farms for food, treasure, and slaves. They are often considered a nuisance by other races, but can prove to be formidable foes in battle due to their numbers and ambush tactics.

### Hunter
Goblin hunters are typically larger and stronger than their kin, with a heightened sense of smell and hearing. They are skilled in tracking and stalking their prey, and are armed with crude yet effective weapons such as spears and bows. They are often used as scouts and trackers by goblin tribes, leading the way on raids and hunts for food. These hunters are also highly valued by goblin chieftains for their ability to provide meat for the tribe. They are feared by other races for their hunting prowess and are considered a greater threat than the average goblin. They may be encountered alone or in small packs, and are known to be more aggressive and intelligent than regular goblins.

### Warchief
Goblin Warchiefs are powerful goblin leaders, known for their strength, intelligence and ruthless nature. They are chosen by their tribes for their ability to lead goblin warriors in battle and raids. They are typically larger and more heavily armed than regular goblins, and are often adorned with trophies from previous battles. They are experts in goblin tactics, able to coordinate ambushes and raids with great efficiency. They are also known to be adept at recruiting other goblin tribes to their cause, creating powerful goblin hordes under their command. Goblin Warchiefs are considered a major threat to other races, and are often the primary target of goblin hunters and adventuring parties. They may also be encountered leading goblin warbands, raiding and pillaging nearby settlements.

### Shaman
Goblin Shamans are powerful goblin spellcasters who wield dark magic and ancient rituals. They are typically found among goblin tribes, serving as spiritual leaders and advisers to the tribe's chief. They are known for their ability to communicate with dark spirits and summon them to aid the tribe in battle. They can also use their magic to curse their enemies, summon monsters, and create illusions. They are often feared and respected by other goblins, who see them as powerful beings with great spiritual power. They also have knowledge of healing magic and can use it to heal the wounds of their fellow goblins. They are known for their distinctive, eerie appearance, often adorned with bones, charms, and other ritualistic items. Goblin Shamans may be encountered leading goblin warbands or providing spiritual guidance to the tribe. They are considered a powerful threat to the adventurers and other races due to their mastery of dark magic.
