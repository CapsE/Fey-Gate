#### Passives
__Trample:__ If the boar moves at least 20ft. in a straight line it can cross creatures of it's size or smaller spaces. When crossing a creatures space that creature looses 2d4 resolve. If it can't it is knocked prone and takes 1d6 blunt damage.

__Fire Resistance:__ The boar doen't loose resolve through fire damage and takes only half as much fire damage.

#### Actions
Attack: +5 to hit. 1d4 resolve / 1d6+3 piercing damage
Move: 30ft.
Intimidate: +5
Break Free: +3
Restore: +3

__Breath Fire__ *(Recharge on 17+)*__:__ Every creature in a 10ft cone in front of the boar takes 3d8 resolve / 3d4 fire damage. A successful Dexterity-Defense halfs this damage.
